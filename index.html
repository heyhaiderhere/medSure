Our Services

Elderly Care

For people who have elders at home, our "elderly care" service is a home-based service that will provide a Profesional and customized treatment according to the patient. Unlike other elder-care services, this service will last for 24 hours. We provide customers with

  1. Skilled nursing
  2. Physical therapy
  3. Speech therapy
  4. Social services or assistance with daily living. Disabled care

Disabled Care

Don't worry if you or anyone in your family have serious disability issue, because now our "disability care" service has professionals that will look after you for 24 hours. We provide

  1. Help with daily needs
  2. Routine based exercises
  3. Daily task assistance

Post operation Care

You might have a patient at home who just had a surgery and is sent back home to recover but you might be busy because of you job or any other reason. But you can noe relax since we are here to provide "post-operation care" services to our customers for 24 hours. Our professionals will help in

  1. Changing the bandages
  2. Providing daily assistance
  3. Preparing post-surgical meals

Parental Care

To prevent complications at the time of childbirth. We provide "pregnancy care" services our customers for 24 hours until the baby is born safely. Our professionals provide

  1. Help to recover from depression and anxiety
  2. Regular weight check
  3. Provide healthy meals
  4. Details on Occiput Anterior Positions (AOP)
  5. Daily task assistance
